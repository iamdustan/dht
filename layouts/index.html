The Davidson Home & Village Tour is a fundraiser for the Davidson Cornelius Child Development Center (DCCDC). The Tour is an opportunity to explore the heart of Davidson through hand-picked homes and beloved village sites. Step inside curated homes to experience a range of diverse architectural styles, inspiring interior design, historical significance, local art displays, and more! Each space will be staffed by a local docent. Come enjoy a self-guided tour and get the inside scoop!

DCCDC provides high-quality early childhood education with scholarship support for children 6 weeks-Pre-K. Nearly 50% of our mini-scholars receive sliding-scale financial assistance ranging from 35-75% of tuition. Proceeds from the Davidson Home & Village Tour fuel tuition scholarships, teacher and staff support, operational costs, and improvements to DCCDC.

FAQ
  • Where do I park?

    Free parking is available throughout the town in designated spaces. The walking tour begins at the Davidson green in front of the Library.

  • Where do I check-in?

    Your first stop is at the Davidson Town Green (119 South Main Street Davidson) where you will check in at the DCCDC tent. There you will receive your tickets required for entry at each home. You will also receive a map and booklet about each of the homes.

  • What is included in the ticket?

    Self-guided walking tour of the homes which includes designated access inside the home as determined by the homeowner.

  • Are pets allowed on the tour?

    While we love pets, they are not permitted on the tour.

  • Can I drive the tour route?

    No, there are not parking spaces available at homes. All houses and sites are selected to be within walking distance from the check in location.

  • What is the tour like for children?

    No strollers will be allowed in the homes, but strollers can be used to walk between homes. Children should be supervised at all times.
